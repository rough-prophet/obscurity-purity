About this album:

Work on this album began shortly after I moved into my new complex in late August. My initial setup was similar to what I had used in the past: a Blue Yeti microphone and a basic DAW, alongside my various cheap Casio/Yamaha keyboards. The Yeti microphone takes primary usage in the earliest track from this album, "Town In Autumn."

Towards the end of fall, I decided to step up my theoretical game and bought a used Focusrite interface. It only has one port, so I adjusted my recording style to a mono setup as opposed to stereo. I incorporated more of my family's old equipment into the process; all the old microphones have certainly seen some wear and tear. The crown jewel is a Kawai KX1 that has only seen use before on some of the old MWBM output.
